About the University

University College of Engineering (UCE), BIT Campus, Anna University, Tiruchirappalli was established in 1999 and functions as a constituent college of Anna University, Chennai. UCE BIT Campus has 7 Faculties, 16 Departments, NBA Accreditation, 10 B.E/B.Tech UG programmes, 8 M.E/M.Tech PG programmes, MBA, MCA, and Ph.D. programmes with 4628 students. It is TEQIP II sponsored with UGC 2f & 12B status. The institute has received ₹300 million in research/consultancy funding. Its objective is to generate highly creative professionals for nation building.

About the Department

The Department of Mathematics, established in 1999, supports UG & PG programmes in Engineering & Technology. It provides strong mathematical foundations and computational skills for industrial/research problems. Faculty research includes Optimization, Queuing Theory, Differential Equations, Fuzzy Mathematics, and Graph Theory.

Aim & Objectives

  • Explore math concepts and models of machine building
  • Transform abstract ideas into efficient, reliable, intelligent machines
  • Promote applications of geometry, robotics algorithms, algebra in control, probability in decision making

Tracks of NCBSMM-25

  • Algebra, Numerical Analysis
  • Operations Research, Differential Equations
  • Mathematical Modeling, Stochastic Modeling
  • Discrete Mathematics, Fluid Mechanics
  • Computational Geometry, Number Theory
  • Mathematical Physics, Cryptography
  • Combinatorics, Topology
  • Machine Learning & Data Science
  • Computational Biology & Bioinformatics
  • Image & Signal Processing
  • Computational Chemistry
  • Theory of Automata
