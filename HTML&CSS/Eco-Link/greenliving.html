Live Sustainably with Eco Link

Tips for Sustainable Living

  • Use reusable bags, bottles, and containers.
  • Choose public transport, bike, or walk when possible.
  • Support local and organic food producers.

DIY Home Energy Saving Ideas

  • Install LED bulbs to reduce energy use.
  • Seal windows and doors to avoid heat loss.
  • Use a smart thermostat to control energy efficiently.

Waste Reduction Practices

  • Compost food waste and garden scraps.
  • Recycle paper, plastic, metal, and glass responsibly.
  • Donate or repurpose old clothes and furniture.

Eco Product Recommendations

  • Bamboo toothbrushes
  • Biodegradable cleaning products
  • Solar-powered chargers
